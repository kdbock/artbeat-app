# Local ArtBeat Consolidated Revenue Overview
## Multi-Stream Artist & Creator Economy Platform

**Document Date**: November 2025  
**Company**: ArtBeat  
**Launch Market**: Eastern North Carolina (Kinston, Greenville, Wilmington)  
**Business Model**: 5-stream revenue ecosystem (Subscriptions, Artwork Sales, Commissions, Gifts, Local Ads)  

---

## Executive Summary

ArtBeat is a **5-revenue stream mobile platform** monetizing the local creator economy in Eastern NC. Rather than relying on a single business model, ArtBeat stacks complementary revenue streams that reinforce each other:

1. **Artist Subscriptions** (recurring) - Professional tools & portfolio platform
2. **Artwork Sales** (transaction) - Direct artist-to-collector marketplace (15% commission)
3. **Direct Commissions** (high-value transaction) - Custom artwork brokerage (15% commission)
4. **Community Gifts** (small transaction) - Direct artist tipping (30% platform margin)
5. **Local SMB Ads** (recurring) - Affordable advertising for regional businesses ($0.99–$9.99 per ad)

**Combined Year 1 Revenue Potential**: **$448K–$1.05M** across all streams  
**Year 3 Projected Revenue**: **$2.1M–$4.2M** (with geographic expansion)  
**Gross Margin**: **72–82%** blended across all streams  
**Path to Profitability**: 18–24 months
