## Risk Mitigation

### Adoption Risk

**Risk**: Artists don't market gifts to supporters; feature remains unknown  
**Mitigation**:

- Founder-led artist outreach (personal calls, demos)
- In-app notifications ("You have supporters ready to send gifts")
- Partner with arts councils for credibility + distribution
- Success stories/case studies ("Local artist earned $1K in gifts")

### Monetization Risk

**Risk**: Gift feature cannibalizes artist subscription sales instead of supplementing  
**Mitigation**:

- Gift revenue reserved for artist payouts (70%), not platform reinvestment
- Market gifts as **appreciation/tips**, not alternative to subscriptions
- Tier messaging: "Support artists today; help them build tools with subscriptions"
- Data: Track and optimize gift feature does NOT reduce subscription adoption

### Regulatory Risk

**Risk**: Apple/Google change IAP policies; custom gift amounts prohibited  
**Mitigation**:

- Preset tiers only (Apple compliant)
- Alternative: Direct payment fallback (less convenient, but available)
- Monitor platform policy changes quarterly

### Churn Risk

**Risk**: One-time gifters don't return; feature feels gimmicky  
**Mitigation**:

- Recipient recognition/thank-you videos (artist gratitude)
- Gifter leaderboards (social status)
- Milestone celebrations ("Your gifts helped this artist gain 1,000 followers!")
- Integration with artist content (gift senders see recipient's new work first)
