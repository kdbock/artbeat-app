# ARTbeat Art Walk - User Experience Guide

A comprehensive guide to using the ARTbeat Art Walk system for discovering, creating, and experiencing interactive art walks with GPS navigation and community features.

## üéØ What is ARTbeat Art Walk?

ARTbeat Art Walk transforms your city into an interactive art gallery. Create custom walking routes that connect public artworks, follow GPS-guided tours with turn-by-turn navigation, discover hidden art treasures, and earn achievements while exploring your community's artistic heritage.

## üöÄ Getting Started

### Quick Start Guide

1. **Open ARTbeat App** ‚Üí Navigate to "Art Walks" section
2. **Discover Local Walks** ‚Üí Browse featured walks in your area

### Understanding Walk Information

**Walk Details Include:**

- **Title & Description**: Overview of the artistic theme and experience
- **Difficulty Rating**: Easy (flat, short), Medium (some hills), Hard (challenging terrain)
- **Estimated Duration**: Time needed including viewing and photography
- **Distance**: Total walking distance between artworks
- **Accessibility**: Wheelchair accessible routes and facilities
- **Public Art Count**: Number of artworks included in the walk
- **Creator Information**: Walk creator and community ratings

## üéÆ Art Walk Experience Features

### Starting an Art Walk

1. **Select Your Walk** from the dashboard or search results
2. **Review Walk Details** including route map and artwork previews
3. **Choose Experience Type**:
   - **Guided Experience**: Full GPS navigation with achievements
   - **Self-Guided**: Basic information without turn-by-turn directions
4. **Tap "Start Walk"** to begin your adventure

### Enhanced Walk Experience (December 2025 Update)

#### Walk Management Menu

Access comprehensive walk controls through the floating menu button:

- **Pause Walk**: Take breaks while preserving your progress
- **Resume Walk**: Continue from where you left off
- **View Progress**: See detailed statistics and completion status
- **Complete Early**: Finish when you've seen enough (available at 80% progress)
- **Abandon Walk**: Exit with confirmation (progress will be lost)

#### Real-Time Progress Tracking

- **App Bar Progress**: See "Walk Name (7/10)" showing current progress
- **Progress Dialog**: Detailed view with:
  - Completion percentage with visual progress bar
  - Art pieces visited count (e.g., "7/10")
  - Photos taken during the walk
  - Duration formatted as hours and minutes
  - Experience points earned so far

#### Smart Exit Handling

- **Active Walk Confirmation**: Confirm when leaving an active walk
- **Progress Preservation**: Your progress is automatically saved
- **Resume Option**: Return to continue your walk later
- **No Confirmation for Completed Walks**: Smooth exit for finished walks

### Navigation Features

- **Turn-by-Turn Directions**: Voice and visual guidance to each artwork
- **Current Location**: Real-time position tracking with accuracy indicators
- **Route Optimization**: Efficient paths between artworks
- **Offline Maps**: Continue navigation even without cellular service

**Artwork Discovery**

- **Proximity Alerts**: Notifications when approaching each artwork

### Achievement System

**Experience Points (XP)**

- **Base Completion**: 100 XP for finishing any walk
- **Perfect Completion Bonus**: +50 XP for visiting all artworks (100%)
- **Speed Bonus**: +25 XP for completing in under 2 hours
- **Photo Documentation Bonus**: +30 XP for taking photos at 50%+ of locations
- **Total Possible**: Up to 205 XP per walk

**Achievement Categories**

- **Distance Milestones**: Bronze (1 mile), Silver (10 miles), Gold (marathon distance)
- **Walk Completion**: Bronze (1 walk), Silver (5 walks), Gold (10 walks), Platinum (50+ walks)
- **Discovery Achievements**: Find hidden art, complete themed walks
- **Social Achievements**: Share walks, engage with community comments
- **Special Events**: Seasonal challenges and limited-time achievements

### Completion Options

#### Standard Completion

- **Visit All Artworks**: Experience the complete curated journey
- **Full Rewards**: Earn maximum XP and achievement progress
- **Complete Experience**: Comprehensive art discovery and education

#### Early Completion (December 2025)

- **Available at 80% Progress**: Option appears when you've visited 80% of artworks
- **Trade-off Explanation**: Clear information about rewards you'll miss
- **Flexible Options**: "Keep Exploring" or "Complete Now"

## üé® Creating Your Own Art Walks

### Walk Creation Process

**Step 1: Planning Your Walk**

- **Choose Your Theme**: Sculptures, murals, historical art, or mixed media
- **Select Geographic Area**: Focus on a neighborhood or district
- **Plan Your Route**: Consider walking distance and difficulty
- **Identify Artworks**: Use the public art database or add new discoveries

**Step 2: Using the Creation Wizard**

- **Walk Information**: Title, description, and cover image
- **Artwork Selection**: Browse and add public art pieces to your walk
- **Route Optimization**: Arrange artworks in logical walking order
- **Difficulty Setting**: Rate your walk's physical challenge level
- **Accessibility Options**: Mark wheelchair accessible routes

**Step 3: Preview and Publish**

- **Route Preview**: View your walk on the map with estimated timing
- **Test Walk**: Take your own walk to verify accuracy
- **Privacy Settings**: Choose public (shareable) or private (personal use)
- **Publish**: Make your walk available to the community

### Advanced Creation Features

**Public Art Integration**

- **Database Search**: Find existing artworks by location or artist
- **Add New Art**: Contribute new public art discoveries
- **Photo Upload**: Add high-quality images of artworks
- **Detailed Information**: Include artist info, installation date, and historical context

**Route Customization**

- **Waypoint Management**: Add stops, viewpoints, or rest areas
- **Distance Optimization**: Automatic route efficiency suggestions
- **Alternative Routes**: Create optional paths for different skill levels
- **Safety Considerations**: Avoid dangerous areas or difficult terrain
