The ARTbeat Settings package creates an intuitive, comprehensive settings management experience that empowers users to customize their ARTbeat journey while maintaining complete control over privacy, security, and preferences. This settings system goes beyond basic configurations to offer personalized experiences that adapt to user behavior, cultural preferences, and security needs.

**Core UX Philosophy:**

- **User Empowerment**: Complete control over app experience and data
- **Privacy First**: Transparent, granular privacy controls with clear explanations
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: Localized experiences that respect cultural differences
- **Progressive Enhancement**: Simple defaults with advanced options for power users
- **Seamless Integration**: Settings that enhance rather than complicate the ARTbeat experience
