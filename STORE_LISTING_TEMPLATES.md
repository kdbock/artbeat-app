**Required:** Yes

**Format:** PNG or JPEG (24-bit, no alpha)  
**File Size:** Max 1 MB

**Design Tips:**

- Showcase your app's key feature
- Use high-quality artwork images
- Include app name/logo
- Keep text minimal and readable
- Use brand colors
- No device frames or screenshots
